This is a second amazing recipe of amazing octopus, inspired by Jian Yang's grandmother's recipes.

Ingredients

  • octopus
  • salt
  • butter
  • thyme

Preparation steps

  1. Heat butter
  2. Grind thyme
  3. Mix butter and thyme
  4. Add octopus
  5. Cook till golden brown
  6. Enjoy!
